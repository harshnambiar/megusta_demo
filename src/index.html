What is Me Gusta?

Me Gusta is a revolutionary decentralized gaming platform offering Play-to-Earn tournaments with real crypto rewards. Our platform seamlessly integrates with multiple blockchains to provide a secure, transparent, and rewarding gaming experience.

Built by gamers for gamers, our ecosystem combines the fun of casual gaming with the benefits of web3 technology, allowing players to truly own their in-game achievements and rewards.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Me Gusta Gaming?

Me Gusta Gaming is a next-generation gaming platform that combines classic arcade gameplay with blockchain technology, offering players unique experiences and rewards.

Do I need a wallet to play games?

No, you can enjoy all our games without a wallet. However, connecting a wallet allows you to earn rewards, save high scores on the blockchain, and access exclusive features.

Which blockchains are supported?

We currently support Ethereum, Mantle, Lisk, Flare, and Gravity chains, with more integrations planned in the future.

How do I earn rewards?

By playing games with a connected wallet, you can earn in-game rewards based on your performance. High scores and achievements are recorded on the blockchain and may qualify for special rewards.

Are the games free to play?

Yes, all games are free to play. Some special events or tournaments may require entry fees in the future, but our core gaming experience will always remain free.

How can I report issues or suggest features?

You can contact our support team via the Discord channel or submit feedback through our official community forums. We value player input and actively incorporate suggestions into our development roadmap.
